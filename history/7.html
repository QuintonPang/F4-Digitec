Working with McDonald's

Founded in 2004, QCK Sneakers is one of the most popular sneaker brands in Malaysia. QCK stands for Quinton, Chin Bao Sheng and Kok Ka Ket, the three young founders of the high quality brand. Over the decades, with the continuous efforts in quality improvement, investment in human resources, products research and development have made us one of the highest quality purveyors of sneakers in the world. At QCK, we pledge to be ethical and responsible in the way we conduct our business. Thus, consumers gain confidence in purchasing our products.

In addition, a solid fan base garnered through our social media and engagement for being a trusted brand in Malaysia. We are available throughout all the channels in modern and general trades as well as e-commerce platforms. Aside from that, our products can also soon be found in various key markets across the world, such as Europe, America, Africa, Japan and the Middle East. We pledge to continue to maintain our standards and deliver superior high standard sneakers to our customers.

Since 2010 the Sneaker Organization has been evaluating the quality of shoes all over the world. Each product is worn and tested individually on numerous parameters carefully selected according to the product category and the expectations of the consumers. Every year, hundreds of evaluation sessions are organised during which the juries take the time to analyse each product individually. The Sneaker Organization testing sessions are based on several parameters applicable to each product. The evaluation sessions are organised over a period of four months during which our juries test a limited number of products per day.
